Private Exclusives

As a private exclusive, your home will not be listed on the MLS for everybody to see. Instead, it is only shown to a private group of Compass agents, giving you more control over what information is shared about your home.

  • Discreet exposure to a curated network of Compass agents
  • Greater control over pricing strategy and market timing
  • Protection of your privacy and personal information
  • Test market interest before going public on the MLS
